Harry potter
The Harry Potter series, written by J.K. Rowling, is one of the most famous fantasy book series in the world. It follows the journey of Harry Potter, a young wizard, and his adventures at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry alongside his friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.
Kasthuri suwada
Kasthuri Suwada" is a well-known Sinhala novel written by Liversack. The book consists of 1,072 pages and comes with a jacket cover.
MadolDuwa
Madol Duwa is a popular Sinhala novel written by Martin Wickramasinghe, one of Sri Lanka's most renowned writers. The story revolves around the adventurous journey of a young boy named Somasiri, who runs away from home to escape his troubled life.
Wool
Wool is the first book in the Silo series. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, humanity is forced to live in a massive underground silo, as the outside world has become uninhabitable. The silo is governed by strict rules and regulations, and people are forbidden to venture outside.
SHift
Shift is the second book in the Silo series by Hugh Howey, following Wool. While Wool introduces readers to the world of the silo and its inhabitants, Shift explores the events leading up to the creation of the silo and the reasons behind the dystopian society.
